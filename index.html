About Us

Lumintu Logic is an IT company that originates from Yogyakarta, Indonesia. The word Lumintu itself means continuous service in Javanese. Founded in 2009, Lumintu Logic developed its first products mostly for desktop application environment.

As we are bound to the principle of continuous service, we realize that we must follow the ongoing development in IT industry. Therefore, we are now providing applications for web environment and continuously adapting into using new frameworks.

We are also maintaining the availability of services by investing in new infrastructures that we mainly use as the home for our SaaS (Software as a Service).
